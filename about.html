HELIPORT COMPANY STORY

At Heliport Co., we believe in taking adventures to new heights.Based and operated in beautiful Vancouver, we take pride in our professional services and deliver only the best since 1998. Founders Finn Strumdrang and Jake Fincher are heliski enthusiast and enjoy exploring unchartered routes on snowy caps. Our main area of focus is at Whistler, BC, home of the 2010 Winter Olympics. We offer three main services: heliski, tours, and private tours/charters. Onboard with us are highly experienced and trained pilots who share similar interests with us. At Heliport Co., we stay true to our mission to "take adventures to new heights." So what are you waiting for? Your adventure awaits you at Heliport Co. We hope to see you soon.
